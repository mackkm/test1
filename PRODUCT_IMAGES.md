# Pawlettes Product Images Guide

## Overview
This guide provides detailed prompts and specifications for generating professional product photos for the Pawlettes Shopify store. All 8 products currently lack images.

## Product Image Specifications
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Resolution**: 1200x1200px (minimum) for product pages
- **Background**: Clean white or light gray background
- **Style**: Professional product photography, well-lit, clean
- **Angle**: 3/4 view or directly from front

## Recommended Tools for Image Generation

### Option 1: AI Image Generators (Fastest)
- **Midjourney**: Best for product photography quality
  - Subscription: $10-120/month
  - Quality: Excellent, professional results
  
- **DALL-E 3**: Good quality, integrated with ChatGPT
  - Pay per image
  - Quality: Very good
  
- **Stable Diffusion**: Open-source, cost-effective
  - Free to use locally
  - Quality: Good with proper prompts

### Option 2: Stock Photo Sites
- Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
- Search for similar pet products
- Free but may need editing

### Option 3: Professional Photography
- Hire a product photographer
- Most authentic and professional
- Higher cost

### Option 4: Hybrid Approach
1. Use AI for initial concepts
2. Professional photographer for hero shots
3. Professional editor for consistency

## Next Steps

1. **Choose image generation tool** based on budget and timeline
2. **Use the prompts above** in your selected tool

5. **Edit for consistency** (colors, shadows, backgrounds)
6. **Upload to Shopify** - store images in `/images/products/` folder structure
7. **Optimize for web** - compress images while maintaining quality

## Shopify Integration

Once images are generated, upload to Shopify:
1. Go to Products section
2. Edit each product
3. Upload main hero image (1200x1200px)
4. Add additional images for different angles/variations
5. Add alt text for each image (SEO important)
6. Set featured image
