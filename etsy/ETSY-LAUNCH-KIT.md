## PART 1 — Shop setup (one-time)

### 1. Shop basics
- **Shop name:** `Pawlette` (if taken, try `PawletteShop`, `ShopPawlette`, `PawletteCo`)
- **Language / Country / Currency:** your locale (e.g. English / US / USD)
- **Title (55 char, shows under shop name):**
  `Personalized Dog Gifts, Bandanas, Portraits & Walk Gear`

### 2. Shop announcement (top of shop page)
> Welcome to Pawlette 🐾 Personalized, made-with-love gear for the dog who owns
> your whole heart — custom bandanas, hand-illustrated pet portraits, cozy
> dog-parent apparel, and thoughtful walk & mealtime essentials. Most items are
> made to order just for you. Free shipping on orders over $40!

### 3. About section (story)
Reuse `storefront-content/pages.json` "Our Story" text. Add 5–10 photos and a
short intro video if you have one (Etsy boosts shops with an About video).

### 4. Shop policies (Etsy → Settings → Policies)
- **Processing time:** In-stock 1–2 business days; personalized 5–10.
- **Shipping:** Free over $40; calculated rates otherwise.
- **Returns/exchanges:** Accepted within 30 days on non-personalized items.
  Personalized/made-to-order = not returnable unless damaged (Etsy lets you set
  this per-listing). Pull details from `storefront-content/policies-and-pages.md`.
- **Payments:** Enable Etsy Payments.

### 5. Shop sections (left-nav categories)
Create these sections and file each listing under one:
- Personalized Apparel
- Bandanas & Accessories
- Custom Pet Portraits
- Walk & Safety
- Mealtime & Enrichment
- Grooming
- Comfort & Beds

### 6. Branding
- **Shop icon:** Pawlette logo (paw + wordmark).
- **Banner:** use a mockup collage (I can generate a banner SVG on request).

## PART 3 — Etsy SEO & launch tips

- **Titles:** front-load the most important keyword; put "Personalized/Custom"
  first for made-to-order items (huge Etsy search demand).
- **Use all 13 tags**, all long-tail multi-word phrases; don't repeat single words.
- **Photos:** Etsy allows 10 per listing — use the mockups now; add lifestyle
  shots later. First photo should be square and clear.
- **Attributes:** fill every attribute (color, occasion, recipient) — they're
  extra search hooks.
- **Personalization:** toggle ON with clear instructions for custom items.
- **Renew/steady listings:** list all 13 in the first week; Etsy favors active shops.
- **Free shipping guarantee:** offer it over $35–40 (US) for the search boost.
- **First sales:** share listings to friends/social to seed early orders +
  reviews (Etsy ranks shops with reviews higher).
- **Etsy Ads:** once live with reviews, start Etsy Ads at $1–3/day on your best
  listings (separate from your Meta ads).
