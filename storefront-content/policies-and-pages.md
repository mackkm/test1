## 2. Returns & Refunds Policy

**Page title:** Returns & Refunds

> We want you and your pup to love every Pawlette purchase. If something isn't
> right, we're here to help.
>
> **30-day satisfaction guarantee**
> Return eligible items within **30 days of delivery** for a refund or
> exchange. Items must be unused, in original condition, and in their original
> packaging.
>
> **Personalized & made-to-order items**
> Custom portraits, embroidered, engraved, and printed-to-order products are
> **final sale** (they're made uniquely for you) — *unless* they arrive
> damaged or defective, in which case we'll replace them free of charge.
>
> **How to start a return**
> 1. Email [support@pawlette.shop] with your order number and reason.
> 2. We'll send return instructions and, where applicable, a return label.
> 3. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund is issued to the
>    original payment method within **5–10 business days**.
>
> **Damaged or wrong item?**
> Send a photo to [support@pawlette.shop] within 7 days of delivery and we'll
> make it right immediately.
>

## 5. FAQ

**Page title:** FAQ

> **How long until my order arrives?**
> In-stock items ship in 1–2 business days; personalized items in 5–10. After
> that, standard delivery is 3–7 business days. See our Shipping Policy.
>
> **How does personalization work?**
> For custom items (bandanas, tees, crewnecks, ID tags, portraits) you'll enter
> the pet's name / details at checkout. For portraits, you'll upload a photo
> after ordering — we'll email you a link.
>
> **What if it doesn't fit?**
> We offer exchanges within 30 days on non-personalized items. Check the size
> guide on each product page before ordering — and message us if you're between
> sizes.
>
> **Are your products safe for pets?**
> Yes. Our feeding and grooming items use food-grade, BPA-free, pet-safe
> materials. Always supervise your pet with any new product.
>
> **Can I change or cancel my order?**
> Contact us within **12 hours** of ordering at [support@pawlette.shop]. Once a
> personalized item enters production we can't change it.
>
> **Do you ship internationally?**
> [Yes — to listed countries / Not yet — currently US & Canada.] See Shipping.
>
> **How do I care for my items?**
> See the Care Guide page for washing and maintenance by product.
>
> **Still have questions?**
> Email [support@pawlette.shop] — we usually reply within one business day.
